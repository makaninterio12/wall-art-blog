Abstract Canvas Prints

Abstract art remains the cornerstone of modern interiors. Large-scale canvas prints with sweeping brushstrokes, muted earth tones, or vibrant splashes command attention instantly. Look for triptych sets or oversized single panels in 40"×60" for maximum drama on your accent wall.

Pro Tip

Hang abstract art at eye level — the center should sit at 57"–60" from the floor, gallery style.

Single-line drawings and contour illustrations of faces, figures, and architecture in black ink on cream backgrounds look stunning in slim frames. Perfect for Scandinavian and Japandi spaces.

No. 05Boho Luxe

Woven Tapestry & Macramé

Textile wall hangings add warmth and acoustic softness that hard-surface art can't match. Handwoven tapestries with earthy tones and geometric patterns are especially beautiful in boho-modern or warm contemporary interiors. Large-scale macramé works as a dramatic focal point in any space.

Pro Tip

Layer a woven hanging over a painted wall — the contrast between texture and flat color creates visual richness.

No. 09Fine Art Feel

Photography Art Prints

High-resolution photography prints — aerial landscapes, intimate street photography, architectural shots — bring a fine art sensibility at an accessible price point. Black and white photography in oversized frames is a perennial classic; moody color landscapes add dramatic depth.

Pro Tip

Buy unframed prints in a tube and frame them locally — custom look at half the cost.

Bold & PlayfulModern Edge

Neon & LED Wall Signs

Flexible LED neon signs have evolved far beyond novelty. Custom word signs, moon phases, palm trees, and abstract shapes in warm white, blush pink, or electric blue are having a major design moment in 2026. These make extraordinary focal points in eclectic and maximalist contemporary spaces.

Pro Tip

Keep everything else on that wall minimal — the sign needs breathing room to truly shine.
